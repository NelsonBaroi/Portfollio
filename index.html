About

I lead the Bangladesh branch of AMT Engineering JSC, a Russian engineering company operating on the Rooppur Nuclear Power Plant — the first nuclear energy project in Bangladesh's history.

My work sits at the intersection of strategic planning, cross-cultural coordination, and operational execution. I manage budgets, contracts, resource allocation, and performance evaluation across teams that span two countries, two languages, and fundamentally different operational cultures.

Before this role, I spent five years in progressively senior administrative and procurement positions on the same project. I know the operation from the ground up — from procurement logistics to director-level strategy.

What Drives Me

I believe operations work is only as good as the decisions behind it. For six years I've managed increasingly complex responsibilities on a project of national significance — and at every level, the challenge has been the same: making the right call with incomplete information, under real constraints, where mistakes cost real money.

That's why I'm investing in analytical capability. Not to change careers — but to sharpen the instrument I already use every day. The best leaders I've worked with aren't the loudest. They're the ones who can see the data behind the noise and act on it with confidence.

I'm currently deepening my skills in SQL, data analysis, and business intelligence tools — building toward a formal qualification in Business Analytics that will complement my operational experience.
