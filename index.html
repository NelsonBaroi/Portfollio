About Me

I am Nelson Baroi. I consider myself an open-minded and creative professional who thrives on diverse perspectives and believes in the power of collaboration to drive innovation. My values center around making a positive societal impact, and I approach life with a strategic, critical-thinking mindset.

Currently, I am at a pivotal point in my academic and professional journey, leveraging my expertise to contribute meaningfully to my field. With a Bachelor's degree in Business Informatics from Kalmyk State University, Russia, and a robust background in project and financial management, I have developed strong problem-solving skills and a passion for operational excellence.

As the Director of the Branch in Bangladesh for AMT Engineering JSC at the Rooppur Nuclear Power Plant, I lead administrative and procurement operations, ensuring efficiency and strategic growth. My previous roles as a Senior Administrator at AMT Engineering JSC and as a Procurement Manager and Administrative Staff at LLC "Freyssinet" have further honed my ability to manage complex projects, optimize resources, and support colleagues in high-stakes environments.

In addition to my leadership and managerial skills, I possess a strong technical foundation in IT and digital tools, including Microsoft Office, Adobe Photoshop, HTML, CSS, PHP, databases, and data visualization. These skills allow me to bridge the gap between business strategy and technology, driving innovation within my organization.

Fluent in Bengali (native), English (C1), and Russian (C1), I excel in cross-cultural communication, fostering collaboration in international business settings. My ability to lead, strategize, and adapt to challenges positions me as a dynamic professional dedicated to making a lasting impact.
