Origins — the detail

Chandraghona sits on the Karnaphuli River in Rangamati, about 50 km from Chittagong city. The town is tied to the Karnaphuli Paper Mills — once the largest paper mill in South Asia — which shaped how the community saw itself.

The Chittagong Hill Tracts are hilly, forested, and politically complex: indigenous communities, ethnic minorities, beauty and marginality together. I am from the Christian minority in Bangladesh — roughly 0.4% of the country. Christian, Hill Tracts, small town: three layers of "not the default" before I ever left home.

That background gave me quiet resilience, belief that education and merit open doors, and comfort being the different one — whether in a Russian lecture hall or on a nuclear construction site.

Education — extended record

Public site has dates. Here is the longer version.

SSC (2009) — Chandraghona, Rangamati

First major public exam in Bangladesh, usually age 15–16. Finishing SSC in the Hill Tracts and still getting into Notre Dame told me early that merit could open doors.

HSC (2011) — Notre Dame College, Dhaka

I left home at sixteen for Notre Dame College, Dhaka — widely regarded as the country's top intermediate college.

Notre Dame was founded in 1949 by the Congregation of Holy Cross. Competitive admissions, strong board results, Motijheel campus in Dhaka's commercial heart. Holy Cross ethos — service, integrity, formation — matched what my family taught me.

Russia (2014–2018) — Kalmyk State University

Russian Government Scholarship after HSC. University in Elista, capital of Kalmykia — Buddhist-majority republic in southern Russia, only such region in Europe. Scholarships assign you where the federation places you; I landed in a culturally unique place.

BSc Business Informatics — management, information systems, economics, systems analysis. Thesis: The Use of Computer Technology for Intensification of Business Processes in the Field of Forecasting. That thesis first showed me data as strategic instrument, not just record — still the thread behind the MSc.

I was the only foreign student in my class. Four years in Russian as the language of instruction, no compatriot buffer. Russian became professional necessity, not a hobby.

Rooppur — project depth

Rooppur Nuclear Power Plant — Ishwardi, Pabna, on the Padma — Bangladesh's first nuclear plant.

Parameter Detail
Capacity 2,400 MW (2 × 1,200 MW)
Reactor VVER-1200 (Generation III+)
Builder Rosatom
Project value ~USD 12.65 billion
Construction From 2017; Unit 1 concrete 2018
Operational Unit 1 fuel loading began May 2026
Significance Bangladesh as world's 33rd nuclear power nation

At peak, 20,000+ people worked the site. 4,500+ Russians live in a purpose-built zone locals call "Little Russia" — Russian signage, restaurants, clinics; Bengali vendors negotiating in Russian. The quiet Padma village became an international township. That is the daily context.

VVER-1200 — Gen III+ PWR, post-Fukushima safety standards, Rosatom's export flagship (also El Dabaa, Egypt, where Atom Tech Alliance works).

AMT Engineering JSC is in Atom Tech Alliance — Russian engineering group supplying auxiliary equipment and services for nuclear plants worldwide.

How I work

Not on philosophy — that page is values. This is behaviour.

Communication: Direct. Short sentences for points, longer for explanation. "Look," "Here's the thing," "Frankly" when it matters. Warm but I will not pad bad news.

Decisions: I use the OODA loop (Observe–Orient–Decide–Act) — Boyd's framework — on serious choices, including visa document strategy.

Planning: Visa portal, 52-week study plan, hotel research with pay rates, application templates months early, this chatbot. Not anxiety — preparation compounds.

Technology: Tool, not identity. Python because analytics jobs demand it; chatbot because it extends reach.

Work rhythm: Early mornings show up in my logs (3–5 AM work blocks). Director job plus self-study — discipline bordering on compulsive.

Risk: Calculated, not gambler — Russia at 19 (scholarship), MSc at peak career (employer backup), hotel work despite director CV (short humility, long gain).

Identity layers: Bangladeshi, Christian minority, Hill Tracts, Russian-educated, nuclear industry, soon Irish academic — I bridge more than I broadcast.
